Protecting Workers Through Award-Winning Design


Problem Statement
Workers operating in harsh industrial environments experience fatigue, discomfort, and safety risks due to traditional welding helmet designs.

Objective
Improve worker safety, comfort, and productivity through innovative product design.

Approach
McKinsey Design and 3M implemented a human-centered design approach by observing welders in real-world environments, gathering feedback, and iteratively refining prototypes.

Solution
The redesigned Speedglas welding helmet focused on ergonomics, comfort, and usability while maintaining high safety standards.

Outcome
The solution enhanced worker experience and earned international design recognition, demonstrating the value of empathy-driven product innovation.
